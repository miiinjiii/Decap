# Trading Card Games

Trading Card Games are one of my favorite hobbies.

I enjoy both collecting cards and actually playing the games with friends.

## Games I Play

* Magic: The Gathering
* Pokémon TCG
* Yu-Gi-Oh!

## Why I Like TCGs

I like TCGs because they combine strategy, creativity, and collecting.

Building decks, learning new combos, and improving over time makes every game different and interesting.

## Favorite Part

My favorite part is deck building.

I enjoy testing different strategies and finding new ways to improve my decks.

I also like collecting cards with great artwork and rare versions.
