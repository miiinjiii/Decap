One of my favorite hobbies is playing Magic: The Gathering, especially the Commander format.

Recently, I built a new Commander deck and I have been really enjoying both the deckbuilding process and testing it in games.

I use [Moxfield](https://moxfield.com/)  to organize my decks and track upgrades because it makes deckbuilding much easier.

## Why I Like Commander

Commander is my favorite MTG format because every game feels different.

You can build around your favorite cards, your favorite colors, or even your favorite playstyle. It feels much more personal than competitive formats.

I especially enjoy decks that have strong synergy, good value engines, and fun interactions instead of only trying to win as fast as possible.

## Future Upgrades

I am still testing possible upgrades for the deck.

Some future improvements could include:

* better mana consistency
* stronger interaction pieces
* smoother win conditions
* stronger late-game value

That is one of the reasons why I enjoy Commander so much — a deck is never really finished.
