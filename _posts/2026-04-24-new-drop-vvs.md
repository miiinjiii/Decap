Recently, on April 22nd one of my favorite groups released a new EP called $TAY THE NIGHT.  

With *$TAY THE NIGHT*, VVS shows once again why they are one of the most exciting groups for me. The EP combines strong rap, catchy beats, and exactly the kind of energy I love in their music.

What I like most about this release is the combination of the members. Each member brings her own style, but together it works perfectly. Especially the rap parts really impressed me this time—they give the songs so much character and make the whole EP even stronger.

My personal favorites are definitely Ilee and Brittany. Both of them delivered once again on this EP and showed why they always stand out to me. Their parts stay in your head immediately and give the tracks even more power. But I also have to say that the other members did an amazing job and complete the group perfectly.

Of course, you can also feel that JiU is missing. She is an important part of the group, and her presence is definitely missed. Still, VVS manages to deliver a strong EP even without her, and it still sounds really good.

For me, *$TAY THE NIGHT* is a release I will definitely keep listening to—strong rap parts, great member chemistry, and exactly the vibe I want from VVS.

* V.V.$
