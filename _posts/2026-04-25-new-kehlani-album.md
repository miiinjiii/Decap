With *KEHLANI*, Kehlani proves once again why she is one of the strongest voices in modern R&B. After “Folded” already set the bar incredibly high by winning two Grammy Awards for Best R&B Song and Best R&B Performance, expectations for this album were huge—but she definitely did not disappoint.  

What I really like about this album is how polished and confident it sounds. Kehlani keeps her emotional songwriting and smooth vocals, but also brings a fresh energy that makes the whole project feel powerful and complete. You can hear how much experience and growth is behind every track.

Another thing that makes this album special is the list of featured artists. Having legends and well-known names like Missy Elliott, Usher, and Lil Wayne on the album adds even more excitement. These features do not feel forced—they fit naturally and help make the project feel even bigger. Missy Elliott especially stands out, and the collaboration feels like a perfect match for Kehlani’s style.

Even with such big names on the tracklist, Kehlani still remains the center of the album. She keeps the balance between strong collaborations and her own personal sound, which is what makes the album work so well.

For me, *KEHLANI* is an album that lives up to the hype—strong vocals, emotional storytelling, huge features, and the proof that “Folded” was only the beginning.

## Favorite Songs

* Folded
* Back and Forth
* I Need You
* Oooh
